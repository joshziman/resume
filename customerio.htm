Design Principles

Simplicity, Usability & Beauty

I agree that these principles represent UX in its highest form. I follow a similar design model, or north star, and I'm certainly open to adapting!

It includes three similar principles structured in a hierarchy. In this model, UX is delivered in three steps: function, ease, and then delight. Sometimes teams can ship all at once, and other times, just one or two. But the guiding rule is to ship in that order.
